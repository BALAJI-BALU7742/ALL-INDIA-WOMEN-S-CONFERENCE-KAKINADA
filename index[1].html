Success Stories

Nagalakshmi, daughter of a Kirana merchant

Nagalakshmi, daughter of a Kirana merchant, who has mobility challenge, uses a wheelchair to move from place to place with help. She has studied up to 10th standard. She comes from a small village called Pedapauram which is 10 kms away from Kakinada.

Nagalakshmi is determined that she wants to live with dignity and not be a burden on anyone. She goes to AIWC Kakinada centre to learn tailoring to be able to earn a living and support her father. Our Kakinada Branch enrolled her in their Udaan project supported by AIWC Head Office, where girls are trained and skill training. We are very happy that we were able to help her and will continue supporting her future endeavours.
